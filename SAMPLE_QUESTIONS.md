## 💡 Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific with department names** — Use full names like "CSE AI ML" or "CSE Data Science" instead of just "AI" or "DS"
2. **Use abbreviations the chatbot knows** — AIDS, CSE-DS, CSE-AI-ML, CSBS, ECE, EEE, BME, PHE, MBA
3. **Ask one question at a time** for the most accurate answer
4. **For faculty queries** — mention the department name: "faculty of CSE AI ML department"
5. **For placement queries** — specify the department if you want department-specific data: "CSE placements" or "ECE placement details"
6. **For HOD queries** — "Who is the HOD of [department name] department?"
